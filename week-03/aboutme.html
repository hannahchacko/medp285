Biography

Welcome to my page! A bit about myself- I consider myself a creative , but outside of school and work, I love taking meditative walks near a pier or around my neighborhood in Ridgewood Queens. A few of my interests that I've always had are jazz (any type), frozen yogurt, and learning languages (I still don't know many.) I like to be a part of community orientated spaces, and spaces that challenge how things can be made easier and accessible for those within the community. This interest is pushes forward my career in UX Design, or any other field that has the common denominator of usability.
